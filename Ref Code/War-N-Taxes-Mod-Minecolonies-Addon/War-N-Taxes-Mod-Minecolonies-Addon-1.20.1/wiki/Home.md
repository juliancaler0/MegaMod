# War 'N Taxes - Minecolonies Addon

Welcome to the **War 'N Taxes** wiki! This comprehensive add-on expands the Minecolonies experience by introducing complex geopolitical mechanics, economy, and dynamic conflict to your Minecraft server or single-player world.

## Mod Overview

War 'N Taxes transforms peaceful city-building into a strategic simulation. Colonies are no longer isolated bubbles; they interact, trade, extort, and wage war against one another. With new administrative systems, you can tax your citizens, engage in espionage, and exert dominance over neighboring factions.

**Core Systems introduced:**
- **Taxation & Economy**: Automatically generates revenue based on your colony's development and citizen happiness. Includes debt management, extortion, and war chests.
- **War & PvP**: Wage war against other colonies, engage in sieges, and claim their wealth or vassalize them. Build your militia and ensure your guard towers are ready.
- **Vassalization System**: Subjugate defeated colonies during wars so they pay you periodic tribute.
- **Espionage**: Send spies to scout, sabotage, bribe, and steal from rival towns.
- **Random Events**: Dynamic events like Merchants, Food Shortages, Plagues, and Festivals keep leadership engaging.
- **Colony Abandonment**: Reclaim, protect, or take over inactive colonies on the server dynamically.
